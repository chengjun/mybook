Doc2VecÂ¶

Le and Mikolov in 2014 introduced the Doc2Vec algorithm, which usually outperforms such simple-averaging of Word2Vec vectors.

https://cs.stanford.edu/~quocle/paragraph_vector.pdf

The basic idea is: act as if a document has another floating word-like vector, which contributes to all training predictions, and is updated like other word-vectors, but we will call it a doc-vector. Gensimâ€™s Doc2Vec class implements this algorithm.

https://radimrehurek.com/gensim/models/doc2vec.html
